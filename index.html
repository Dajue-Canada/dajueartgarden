    DAJUE NEWS

DAJUE CULTURE is a comprehensive multinational company "rooted in traditional culture and grounded in contemporary life." Established in 2018 and headquartered in Vancouver, Canada, the company advocates for using Eastern traditional culture as a medium to explore new trends in Zen-inspired living through cultural integration, and is committed to promoting the exchange and fusion of Eastern and Western cultures and arts.
In the field of garden art, we have meticulously crafted three distinct sub-brands: DAJUE ART GARDEN CENTRE, DAJUE GARDEN & FLOWERS, and DAJUE COURTYARD. Each sub-brand carries the spiritual core of Dajue Culture, providing customers with comprehensive gardening services. We aim to achieve harmony between nature and art in every detail, making your living space vibrant and full of vitality.
