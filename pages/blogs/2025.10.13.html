October Garden Guide: Colorful Blooms and Fall Prep in Vancouver

October in Vancouver is a wonderful month for gardeners to enjoy the beauty of autumn while preparing for the months ahead. The cooler weather and crisp air set the perfect stage for fall blooms such as chrysanthemums, asters, and black-eyed Susans, which bring vibrant pops of colour to gardens and patios. It's also a good time to plant spring-flowering bulbs like tulips, daffodils, and crocuses, ensuring a cheerful display when winter fades.


For those who enjoy container gardening, ornamental kale and pansies are excellent choices to refresh planters with long-lasting colour. Gardeners can also take this opportunity to tidy up spent summer plants, enrich the soil with compost, and protect perennials with mulch.

Whether you're looking to brighten your space with seasonal flowers or plan ahead for spring, October offers the perfect balance of beauty and preparation. Visit our garden centre to discover plants and ideas to inspire your fall garden.
