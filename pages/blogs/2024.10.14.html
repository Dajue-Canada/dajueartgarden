Popular Indoor Tropicals for the Fall Season in Vancouver

As the cooler weather sets in, bringing a touch of the tropics indoors can instantly liven up your space. Tropical plants thrive in the warm, controlled environment of your home, and their lush greenery provides a vibrant contrast to Vancouver’s fall weather. Here are some popular indoor tropical plants perfect for the fall season.



1. Monstera Deliciosa (Monstera deliciosa)

Known for its iconic, split leaves, the Monstera Deliciosa is a favorite for indoor spaces. This low-maintenance plant thrives in indirect light, making it ideal for Vancouver’s shorter days. It also adds a dramatic tropical flair to any room.


2. Fiddle Leaf Fig (Ficus lyrata)

The Fiddle Leaf Fig is known for its large, dramatic leaves that can create a stunning focal point in any room. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and can adapt to lower light conditions. Its bold, sculptural shape makes it a perfect addition to your indoor tropical collection, bringing an elegant touch to your fall decor.


3. Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica)

The Rubber Plant is loved for its glossy, dark green leaves and robust growth. It thrives in bright, indirect light and can tolerate some neglect, making it a low-maintenance option for busy households. Its attractive foliage not only enhances the beauty of your space but also contributes to improved air quality.


4. ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

The ZZ plant is known for its glossy, dark green leaves and its resilience in low light conditions. It’s perfect for fall as it requires minimal watering, making it ideal for those who might be too busy to maintain high-care plants.


5. Philodendron (Philodendron spp.)

The heart-shaped leaves of Philodendron varieties, like the Philodendron Brasil, add a lush, tropical vibe indoors. These plants are easy to care for and thrive in various lighting conditions.


Embrace Vancouver’s cozy fall season with these tropical beauties, and enjoy their lush, year-round greenery!
