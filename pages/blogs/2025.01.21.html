Celebrate Lunar New Year with Bright Blooms


The Lunar New Year is a time of joy, renewal, and togethernes. As families gather to celebrate and usher in good fortune for the coming year, flowers play an integral role in setting the festive mood. We offer a selection of stunning blooms that not only beautify your home but also carry meaningful blessings for the season. Let's explore some of the most popular Lunar New Year flowers and their symbolisms.


1. Orchids: Grace and Elegance


Orchids are a timeless favorite during the Spring Festival. Symbolizing elegance, beauty, and prosperity, orchids are a meaningful gift and a perfect addition to any home. Their delicate yet bold blooms create a sophisticated atmosphere, making them a centerpiece of festive decorations.


2. Peonies: Wealth and Prosperity


Known as the "king of flowers," peonies are cherished for their lush, full blossoms and vibrant colors. They represent wealth, honor, and prosperity, making them a must-have for welcoming good fortune in the new year. Choose red or pink peonies for an extra auspicious touch.


3. Kumquat Trees: Luck and Abundance


Kumquat trees are more than just decorative; they are a symbol of wealth and abundance. The bright golden fruits resemble tiny oranges, which are associated with wealth and happiness. Display a kumquat tree in your living room to invite good fortune into your home.


4. Lucky Bamboo: Harmony and Growth


Lucky bamboo is a popular choice for the Spring Festival due to its association with resilience, strength, and growth. Arranged in auspicious numbers, such as three for happiness or eight for wealth, lucky bamboo brings positive energy to your space.


5. Plum Blossoms: Strength and Renewal


The delicate yet hardy plum blossom is a beloved symbol of perseverance and renewal. Blooming in the cold of winter, these flowers embody resilience and hope, qualities that are especially meaningful during the Spring Festival.


6. Chrysanthemums: Longevity and Happiness


Chrysanthemums are a versatile flower that signifies longevity and happiness. Their cheerful colors and intricate petals make them a popular choice for brightening up homes and bringing festive cheer.
