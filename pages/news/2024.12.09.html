Creating Holiday Magic: Christmas Floral Workshop at DAJUE Garden & Flowers

On December 6th, DAJUE Garden & Flowers hosted a festive Christmas Floral Workshop from 10:30 AM to 12:00 PM PST. This engaging event welcomed participants eager to learn the art of arranging Christmas flowers to create their own holiday-inspired decorations.


Under expert guidance, attendees discovered techniques for designing beautiful floral arrangements perfect for the festive season. The workshop provided hands-on experience, allowing participants to craft their unique creations while enjoying the holiday spirit. Refreshments were served, creating a cozy, welcoming environment for mingling and creativity.


At the end of the session, everyone left with their own floral arrangement masterpiece—ready to adorn their homes for the holidays. The event was a wonderful way to celebrate the season, connect with the community, and share the joy of creative holiday decor.


DAJUE Garden & Flowers continues to bring inspiring workshops to the local community, blending beauty, creativity, and tradition.
