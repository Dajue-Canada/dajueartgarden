Easter Bloom & Bond Workshop Highlights

On April 19, DAJUE Garden & Flowers hosted a heartwarming Easter Bloom & Bond Workshop, bringing together five pairs of moms and children for a special afternoon of creativity and connection. The event was a beautiful celebration of spring and family, where each participant had the chance to design and craft their very own floral bouquet.


The workshop space was filled with the cheerful colors and fresh scents of seasonal blooms. Under the guidance of the friendly floral team, both moms and kids learned how to select, arrange, and style their flowers into stunning springtime arrangements. It was a joy to see the little ones carefully placing flowers, working side by side with their mothers, creating not just bouquets but beautiful memories.


The event created a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families could relax, bond, and share quality time together away from their busy routines. By the end of the afternoon, everyone left with their finished bouquets and happy hearts.

Workshops like these are a lovely reminder of how simple activities can bring people closer. DAJUE Garden & Flowers continues to offer meaningful, family-friendly experiences, and this Easter event was yet another example of their thoughtful community gatherings.
