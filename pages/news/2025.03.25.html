DAJUE Art Garden Centre Grand Opening April 4

DAJUE Art Garden Centre will officially open on April 4, 2025! As a paradise for gardening enthusiasts in Richmond, Vancouver, we have carefully prepared a wide selection of high-quality plants and gardening supplies to bring more vitality and greenery to your spring and summer garden.


Due to ongoing construction, this opening period will continue until the end of July. During this time, we will continuously introduce new arrivals, ensuring you can find your favorite flowers, tropical plants, gardening tools, and unique home greenery.


 We sincerely invite gardening enthusiasts in Richmond, Vancouver to visit and explore our collection.

 Stay connected with us through our website and social media for the latest updates and special promotions!


Address: 8451 No. 5 Road, Richmond

Opening Dates: April 4, 2025 – End of July

                          Open Daily - 10 AM - 6 PM

Products Available: Annuals, Perennials, Tropical plants, garden flowers, gardening tools, home décor greenery, and more
